# Audio Files Required

Place the following audio files in this folder:

## Background Music (MP3)
- `bgm_farm.mp3` - Main background music (looping, relaxing farm ambience)

## Sound Effects (WAV)
- `sfx_hover.wav` - Button hover sound (soft, subtle)
- `sfx_click.wav` - Button click sound (satisfying click)
- `sfx_popup.wav` - Popup/dialog appear sound (notification chime)
- `sfx_purchase.wav` - Purchase/buy sound (cash register or coin drop)
- `sfx_plant.wav` - Planting seed sound (digging/soil)
- `sfx_water.wav` - Watering sound (water splash)
- `sfx_harvest.wav` - Harvest sound (pluck/pull)
- `sfx_success.wav` - Success/achievement sound (cheerful jingle)
- `sfx_coins.wav` - Receiving coins sound (coin jingle)
- `sfx_error.wav` - Error/fail sound (soft buzz)

## Recommended Sources (Free)
- Freesound.org (free with attribution)
- OpenGameArt.org (free game assets)
- Pixabay.com (royalty-free sounds)
- Zapsplat.com (free with account)

## Format Requirements
- BGM: MP3 format, 128-320kbps
- SFX: WAV format, 44.1kHz, 16-bit
- Keep file sizes reasonable (SFX < 500KB each, BGM < 5MB)

## Volume Recommendations
- BGM should be calm and not distracting (will play at 30% volume)
- SFX should be clear but not jarring (will play at 50% volume)
